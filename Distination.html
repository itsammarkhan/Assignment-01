Explore The Best Places To Visit In Skardu


Shangrilla (Upper Kachura) Lake

Shangrila Lake, also known as Lower Kachura Lake, is a breathtaking natural wonder located near Skardu
in Pakistan's Gilgit-Baltistan region. The heart-shaped lake is surrounded by lush meadows and towering
mountains, creating a picturesque setting. Visitors can enjoy the serene Shangrila Resort, explore the rich Balti
culture, and embark on adventures in the nearby Karakoram Range, making it a multifaceted destination.


Shigar Valley

Shigar Valley in Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan, is a pristine Himalayan paradise renowned for its lush landscapes,
traditional villages, and historic Shigar Fort. This picturesque valley offers a glimpse into the rich Balti culture
and is a gateway to exploration in the surrounding Karakoram Range, including treks to some of the world's highest peaks.


Khaplu Valley

Khaplu Valley, nestled in the Skardu district of Gilgit-Baltistan, is a hidden gem offering breathtaking landscapes, serene rivers, and a tranquil ambiance. Visitors can explore the historic Khaplu Fort, which stands as a testament to the region's cultural heritage, while also enjoying the stunning vistas of surrounding peaks like Masherbrum and Saltoro Kangri.


Basho Valley

Basho Valley, located in Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan, is a remote and pristine haven for nature enthusiasts. Surrounded by majestic mountains and lush meadows, it offers a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. This hidden gem is ideal for trekking, camping, and immersing in the untouched beauty of the Karakoram Range.


Deosai National Park

Deosai National Park, often referred to as the "Land of Giants," is a high-altitude plateau in Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan. It's known for its vast, rolling meadows, unique wildlife, and stunning landscapes. Visitors can spot the Himalayan brown bear and other rare species while taking in the breathtaking views of the Deosai Plains.
