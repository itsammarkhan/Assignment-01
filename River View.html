SKARDU RIVER VIEW

The Skardu River, or Shigar River, is surrounded by towering mountains, including some of the world's highest peaks like K2. The river's turquoise waters wind through the valley, creating a serene and captivating landscape. You can find several viewpoints and vantage points in and around Skardu from where you can enjoy the mesmerizing river views.

One of the popular spots to admire the Skardu River is the Shangrila Resort, also known as "Heaven on Earth." It's located on the banks of the Shigar River and provides a peaceful setting with lush gardens and stunning river views. The Shangrila Resort offers a serene environment where you can relax and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.
